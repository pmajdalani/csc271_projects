Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish featuring vinegared rice, often combined with raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and other ingredients. It is commonly accompanied by soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. There are several types of sushi, including nigiri (hand-pressed rice topped with fish), maki (rolled sushi with seaweed), and sashimi (sliced raw fish served without rice). Known for its delicate balance of flavors and artistic presentation, sushi is enjoyed both as a casual meal and in fine dining settings.

Nate's Favorite Dish: Chicken Parm

Chicken Parmesan, or Chicken Parmigiana, is a popular Italian-American dish featuring breaded chicken cutlets that are fried to a crispy golden brown, then topped with marinara sauce and melted cheese. Traditionally, mozzarella and Parmesan cheese are used, and the dish is baked to perfection until the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. It is often served with a side of pasta or vegetables and is known for its rich, comforting flavors, combining the savory chicken with the tangy sauce and creamy cheese.
